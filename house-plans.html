House Plans Sites

My role: Visual design

Hanley Wood has a portfolio of various brands for selling house plans online. All share a basic, common structure, and are differentiated by visual design. They asked me to produce designs for two of their brands, BuilderHousePlans and Select Home Designs.

BuilderHousePlans was to follow the style of Builder Online, Hanley Wood's trade publication for the construction industry. Select Home Designs had no other existing website design, so I came up with an original style for this mockup.

The management was pleased with these designs but elected to hold off indefinitely from producing them. Ultimately all of the house plans sites were moved to a simplified successor architecture.
