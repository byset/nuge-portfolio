American Interest website

visual design
Click on an image to enlarge.

I created planning documents, site maps, wireframes, and mockups for a redesign of the American Interest website.

The redesign was deployed in a piecemeal fashion. The blogs made it to production first and looked much like the mockups shown here. I implemented the design myself in HTML/CSS and integrated it into a Wordpress theme. Walter Russell Mead's popular blog sported this appearance for a good while.

The home page design went through some further iterations and the design shown here didn't quite survive. The entire site has since been redesigned.
