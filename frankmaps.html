The Origins of Political Order maps

print design
Click on an image to enlarge.

Francis Fukuyama needed nine maps for his upcoming book The Origins of Political Order, which would be published in 2011 by Farrar, Straus and Giroux. With guidance from Dr. Fukuyama and FSG's editorial staff, I created nine maps using Adobe Illustrator.

I sourced vectors of basic landforms, lakes, and rivers from public domain and commercial shapefiles.

Dr. Fukuyama was happy with the results, and would later ask me to help him formats charts to the second (and final) book of the series.
