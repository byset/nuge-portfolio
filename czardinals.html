Czardinals logo

print design
Click on an image to enlarge.

A friend who worked at the Office of National Drug Control Policy asked me to create a design for its softball team, the Czardinals (named after the Drug Czar, who heads the ONDCP). To do so, I created a sort of mashup of the seal of the Executive Office of the President, of which ONDCP is a part, with a cartoon cardinal from a late-50s scorecard.

With the help of the trusty free font Lobster, the Czardinals' logo was born.

The Czardinals made news in 2011 by canceling scheduled games against a team of drug-policy reformers called the One-Hitters.
