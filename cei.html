CEI Website

UXvisual design
Click on an image to enlarge.

The Competitive Enterprise Institute is a think tank and advocacy group. As a contractor for an interactive firm called ReadyMadeWeb, I produced these mockups as part of a project to redesign the CEI’s website. ReadyMadeWeb had already worked out the UX and information architecture, and employed a developer who would translate my design into HTML/CSS for a Drupal template. My only job, then, was to produce desktop-sized design mockups.

I created mockups in several different styles for CEI to choose from. After meeting with CEI staffers to get their thoughts and suggesitions, I created three styles, and after CEI chose one they preferred, I refined it further based on their feedback. The result of this process is shown here. With my part done, the finished mockups were sent on through the development process.
