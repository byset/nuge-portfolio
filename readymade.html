Beltway Nonprofit Site for ReadyMadeWeb

My role: Visual design

As a contractor for an interactive firm called ReadyMadeWeb, I produced these mockups as part of a project to redesign the website of a DC-based think tank and advocacy group. ReadyMadeWeb had already worked out the information architecture and produced rough wireframes. My only job, then, was to create desktop-sized design mockups to pass on to their Drupal developer.

I created mockups in several different styles for the client to choose from. After meeting with their staffers to get their thoughts and suggesitions, I created three styles. After the client chose one they preferred, I refined it further based on their feedback. The final mockups are shown here.
